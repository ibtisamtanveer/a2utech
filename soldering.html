SOLDERING SOLUTIONS

The organization working at board level electronics should need soldering solutions to stuff components on PCBs. We provide the best solutions for soldering, de-soldering and reworking on PCBs to our clients.

1. Soldering Stations

We provide complete solutions of soldering stations to work on all type of boards and all type of ICs. With the range of tips you can achieve the maximum possible ability to work on all type of circuit boards.



2. De-soldering Stations

We provide solutions for de-soldering of TO Components. With the range of tips one can easily de-sold any side of component from boards.

3. Reworking Systems

Reworking machines can solder and de-solder any type of IC package and gives a complete end to end solution to customer. We provide complete solution as per the application of the customer to achieve maximum output from reworking system.

4. Fume Extraction Solutions

We provide complete solutions for fume extraction in labs. These fume extractors are useful to extract the harmful fumes which can be dangerous for user while soldering or de-soldering.
