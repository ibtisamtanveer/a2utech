POWER SOLUTIONS

We are providing end to end solutions for power to our clients. With increasing issues in Pakistan due to the power sector, the products to fulfill the power needs are the most important part of any organization. We not only provide the standard products but solutions to our clients for the problems they face during the operations due to power failure.

1. UPS (Uninterrupted Power Supplies)

We provide Uninterrupted Power Solutions (on-line and line interactive UPS) to clients which allows the appliances and apparatuses run smooth in case of power failure. We can provide solution from 500VA to 100KVA UPS for house hold, labs, hospitals, offices and industries.



2. Batteries

We provide Uninterrupted Power Solutions (on-line and line interactive UPS) to clients which allows the appliances and apparatuses run smooth in case of power failure. We can provide solution from 500VA to 100KVA UPS for house hold, labs, hospitals, offices and industries.

3. Solar Solutions

We have a vast experience of solar solution designing, installation and commissioning. We provide solar solutions for domestic customers, offices and industries as well. Customization is available as per customer demand i-e; Grid Tie Solution, Hybrid Solution or standalone solution.

4. AVR (Automatic Voltage Regulators)

We provide Automatic Voltage Regulators of wide range having application in houses, offices and industries for the safety of the critical equipment.
